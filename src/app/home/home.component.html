Welcome! Track the financial value of your collection! To Get Started 1) Click "Decks" on the top

3) Add some cards
4) Click "Deck Overview" on the list on the right side
5) Hit the "Refresh" button
6) Click "Dashboard" on the top
7) You should see a graph with one data point on it, come back tomorow and watch your graphs grow!
