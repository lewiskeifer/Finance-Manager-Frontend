Welcome to MTG Pilot! Track the financial value of your collection!

To Get Started 1) Click "Decks" on the top

2) Create a new deck

3) Add some cards

4) Click "Deck Overview" at the top of your deck list

5) Hit the "Refresh" button in the center

6) Click "Dashboard" on the top

7) You should see a graph with one data point on it, come back tomorow and watch your graphs grow!
